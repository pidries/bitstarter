The pond sensor allows you to monitor the water quality of your pond anywhere in the world right from your mobile phone. It connects wirelessly to your router and submits it's data with frequent intervals (15 minutes). It runs on one single AA-battery and can run up to 6 months. Using the online web-interface, you can view the current quality levels and compare them to historical data.

The pond sensor will monitor the pH level, kH level and will indicate if there are floating algae in your pond. There is also a temperature sensor that will inform you when you can start feeding your fish. Using alerts, you can prevent the plants from dying and your fish from getting ill. The pond sensor will make your pond upkeep much easier.

FAQ

Can the pond sensor be used for indoor aquariums?
Yes, the pond sensor can be used in ponds and aquariums.
Can the battery be replaced.
We use a standard AA-battery that can be replaced by the users themselves.
